Booking Availability

Reservations are released monthly on the 1st for the following 90 days. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

Party Size

Our online booking system accommodates parties of up to 10 guests. For larger groups, please contact us at 03 8616 8010 or reservations@nerostable.com

Cancellation Policy

We require 48 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

Sunday Surcharge

A 10% surcharge applies to all bookings on Sundays.

Dress Code

We maintain a smart casual dress code. We kindly ask guests to avoid activewear, beachwear, and thongs.

Guest Policy

Nero's Table is a sophisticated dining experience designed for guests aged 16 years and over. We do not offer children's menus, and are unable to accommodate prams or highchairs.
